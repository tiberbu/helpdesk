## Research Summary

Comprehensive competitive analysis of 15 world-class helpdesk/support tools conducted. Full deliverable written to `docs/competitive-analysis.md`.

### Tools Researched
1. Zendesk — Market leader, enterprise features
2. Freshdesk — SMB-friendly, gamification
3. Intercom — Conversational support, AI leader (Fin)
4. ServiceNow — Enterprise ITSM, ITIL gold standard
5. Jira Service Management — Developer-centric, Atlassian ecosystem
6. HubSpot Service Hub — CRM-integrated support
7. Zoho Desk — AI assistant (Zia), best value
8. Help Scout — Simple, human-centric
9. Kayako — Unified conversations (declining)
10. Front — Shared inbox, collaboration, AI QA
11. Hiver — Gmail-based helpdesk
12. Gladly — People-centered (not ticket-centered)
13. Gorgias — Ecommerce focused (Shopify)
14. Kustomer — CRM + helpdesk fusion
15. Dixa — Conversational, native voice

### Key Findings

1. **AI Agents are the defining feature of 2025–2026** — Intercom Fin leads at 66% autonomous resolution
2. **Frappe Helpdesk has critical gaps** in AI, live chat, omnichannel, automation, and analytics
3. **Frappe's structural advantages** (open-source, no per-agent pricing, Frappe Framework) are significant
4. **Recommended positioning**: "Open-source helpdesk with AI superpowers"
5. **Priority roadmap**: Canned responses, internal notes, collision detection → Live chat, automation → AI features
